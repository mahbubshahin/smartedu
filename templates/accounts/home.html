frequently asked questions

Welcome to the WMES Online Admission FAQs! Here, you'll find answers to the most common questions about the online admission process, the courses we offer, and how you can join the WMES community. We aim to make your experience as smooth and informative as possible. Whether you're a prospective student, a parent, or simply exploring options, we’ve got you covered with helpful details about our admission process, payment methods, and other essential queries. If you don’t find the answer you’re looking for, feel free to contact us directly.

Our program offers a one-year Master's degree in Environmental Science and Management, structured into three semesters, each lasting four months. Classes are conducted on Fridays, with optional online sessions on Saturdays if additional classes are necessary.

Applying for admission is simple and straightforward. Just visit our admissions page, click on the "Apply Now" button, complete the application form, and submit the required documents. Our process is designed to be user-friendly, ensuring a hassle-free experience for all applicants.

To apply for the WMES online admission, simply visit our admissions page and click Apply Now , fill out the required form, and submit your documents. Our easy-to-follow application steps ensure a seamless process.

We support the following payment methods:
  • Bkash - The total fee is BDT 1011 (inclusive of all charges).
  • DBBL Rocket - The total fee is BDT 1000 (inclusive of all charges).
Both Bkash and DBBL Rocket offer a secure and convenient way to pay your admission fee.

Here is the list of offered courses. View Offered Courses

We offer the following degree program:
  • Master in Environmental Scieces and Management – 36 credits

The program spans a duration of one year, divided into three semesters. Each semester lasts for four months.

Regular classes are held throughout the day on Fridays. If additional sessions are required, they will be conducted online on Saturdays.

Semester final examinations are typically scheduled for both Fridays and Saturdays.

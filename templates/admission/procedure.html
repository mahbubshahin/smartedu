Admission Test Details
  • An admission test in MCQ method will be taken based on basics in environmental knowledge. The medium of admission test is English. This MCQ Test will take place in the department.
  • Candidates will have to attend an interview after the admission test on the same day.
  • Missing any of the exams (MCQ and Interview) will result in disqualification.
  • Final selection will be made based on performance in the admission test, previous academic records, and experiences.

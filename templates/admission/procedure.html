Applications are invited from the prospective students desiring to have a one-year Weekend Masters in Environmental Sciences and Management consisting of three trimesters which are offered by the Department of Environmental Sciences, Jahangirnagar University. This program provides the degree of Masters of Science in Environmental Sciences and Management for those who have completed a Bachelor’s degree and want to obtain extensive knowledge and skills in environmental management and sustainability.

Eligibility Criteria for admission in WMES Program
Additional Information

The WMES program seeks applicants with a strong academic background, prioritizing those with a minimum of a 4-year Bachelor’s degree or equivalent Master’s credentials in relevant fields. Applicants are expected to have consistently maintained good academic standing, and those with degrees or professional experience in environmental science or related disciplines will receive additional consideration.

  • Minimum 4 years Bachelor’s (Honors) Degree or 3 years honours and 1 year Master’s degree in any relevant subject/discipline from any accredited institute.
  • BSc (Engineering) in any discipline from any recognized University
  • Academic degree or Professional experience relevant to environmental science/ study/engineering/management/policy/compliance will get priority
  • N.B. Candidates with third Division/Class or CGPA below 2.50 at any level of their academic career are not eligible to apply.
Major Area
  • Industrial Health, Safety and Environmental Management (IHSEM)
  • Industrial Ecology, Waste Management and Wastewater Treatment (IEWWT)
  • Climate Change and Disaster Management (CCDM)
  • Ecology, Biodiversity and Nature Conservation (EBNC)

Admission Test Details
  • An admission test in MCQ method will be taken based on basics in environmental knowledge. The medium of admission test is English. This MCQ Test will take place in the department.
  • Candidates will have to attend an interview after the admission test on the same day.
  • Missing any of the exams (MCQ and Interview) will result in disqualification.
  • Final selection will be made based on performance in the admission test, previous academic records, and experiences.
Admission Test Syllabus
  • Environmental Issues (Bangladesh & Global)
  • Basics of Environmental Science
  • Environmental Law and Policy
  • Consequences of Development/Industrial Activities on Environment
  • Basic Day-to-Day Science
Our program is structured to offer an exceptional foundation for student success, blending academic rigor with real-world relevance. Students benefit from the guidance of highly qualified faculty, many of whom bring international experience and specialized knowledge in their fields. This global perspective enhances learning, helping students gain a deeper understanding of both local and international environmental challenges. The program also emphasizes practical skills and research, providing students with hands-on opportunities to apply what they've learned. Through a supportive learning environment and a curriculum aligned with current industry standards, we prepare our graduates to excel in their careers and make meaningful contributions to the field.
Program Features
  • Highly qualified faculty members with international teaching and research exposures
  • Trimester system to complete the program in the shortest possible time
  • Classes are conducted on weekends only
  • Air-conditioned classrooms equipped with multimedia facilities & sound systems
  • Seminar library with the latest books and journals
  • Transport facilities for off-campus students (all possible routes for Dhaka residents)
  • Degree completion with minimum cost

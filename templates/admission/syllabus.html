Syllabus

Sessions: Spring 2024 - Fall 2030

To obtain the Master of Science (MSc) degree in Environmental Sciences and Management under the Weekend Program, a student is required to complete a total of 36 credit hours, structured as follows:
Semester 1:Five (5) prescribed courses of 3 credit hours each (total 15 credits).
Semester 2:Five (5) elective courses of 3 credit hours each (total 15 credits).
Semester 3: A thesis or module course worth 5 credit hours, and a viva voce worth 1 credit (total 6 credits).
